What is Text Sumarization ?

Text Sumarization refers to the technique of shortening long pieces of text. The intention is to create a coherent and fluent summary having only the main points outlined in the document.

What does our Text Summarizer do ?

Our Text Summarizer takes the massive block of text uploaded and provides the main headings present.
